Spanish for beginners

I am specialized in new learners. The language is transmitted through practical examples with active participation of the learners in order to assimilate in a global way the words, the grammatical or phonetic rules according to their uses and in a usual context, ranging from an informal language to a form written complex. Classes are built around oral communication to enhance the interactivity of learners.

About Lydia Arranz!

I am a Spanish teacher from Madrid (Spain), certified by Instituto Cervantes and with more than 10 years of experience teaching in academies, at the university and one to one lessons in Brazil, Vietnam, Greece and Turkey. I am specialized in online lessons for children and adults (all levels), and I provide personalized lessons. I always adapt my materials to the students needs. I teach Spanish in an interactive and fun way and I take advantage of every minute of my classes to make sure that all my students progress as they should.
